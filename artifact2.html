Artifact 2: AI/ML Timeline Presentation

A comprehensive, visually engaging timeline presentation showcasing the evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies from inception to present-day breakthroughs.

← Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

This artifact is a timeline presentation crafted to illustrate the milestones in AI and ML history, highlighting seminal research, key innovations, and significant shifts in technology paradigms. It helps viewers understand the rapid progress and critical phases of AI development.

Timeline Highlights

  • 1950s - The Dawn of AI: Alan Turing introduces the Turing Test; early symbolic AI programs developed.
  • 1960s-70s - Expert Systems: Development of rule-based expert systems for decision making.
  • 1980s - Machine Learning Emerges: Introduction of neural networks and backpropagation.
  • 1997 - Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov: Milestone in AI beating a world chess champion.
  • 2010s - Rise of Deep Learning: Breakthroughs in image recognition, speech processing, and natural language understanding.
  • 2020s - AI Democratization: Wider adoption of AI models and frameworks, including transformer architectures.

Methodology

The presentation was created using a step-by-step approach combining research, design, and visualization tools:

  1. Research: Compiled data from academic papers, historical records, and trusted online AI resources.
  2. Organizing Content: Selected and ordered key events chronologically with annotations explaining their significance.
  3. Design: Utilized presentation software to design a visually appealing, easy-to-navigate timeline.
  4. Enhancements: Added images, icons, and brief descriptions to improve engagement and comprehension.
  5. Review and Feedback: Incorporated peer and instructor feedback to refine content accuracy and layout.

Key Learnings

This artifact helped me develop a deeper understanding of AI's historical development and how technological advancements shape current AI applications.

  • Recognized the gradual evolution from symbolic AI to data-driven machine learning.
  • Learned about key pioneers and their contributions to the field.
  • Appreciated the impact of technological breakthroughs such as deep learning and transformers.
  • Improved my presentation design and storytelling skills.
