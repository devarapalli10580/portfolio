Artifact 2: AI & ML Timeline Presentation

This artifact demonstrates my ability to synthesize AI & ML advancements into a structured timeline using visualization and design tools.

Explore Artifact 2

Introduction

Artifact 2 is an interactive timeline that illustrates the evolution of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) from its inception to the present day. The project was designed to provide learners and stakeholders with an easy-to-understand overview of the key milestones in AI development, enabling better comprehension of the technological landscape.

Objectives & Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the historical context and evolution of AI & ML technologies.
  • Apply data visualization principles for educational content delivery.
  • Enhance research and summarization skills.
  • Ability to organize complex data into a structured visual format.
  • Improved design thinking for educational tools.
  • Demonstrated technical and creative skills using presentation tools.

Tools & Technologies

Tools Used: Microsoft PowerPoint, Adobe Illustrator, Canva

Process & Steps

Gathered data from academic journals, research papers, and trusted AI blogs to identify key milestones.

Structured timeline in PowerPoint with a clean, engaging design theme for maximum readability.

Peer-reviewed and updated timeline to ensure accuracy and clarity before submission.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge Solution
Data overload Filtered data to include only key impactful events.
Design complexity Used templates and consistent color codes for readability.

Reflection

This project helped me develop strong research and visualization skills. It enhanced my understanding of how AI evolved and strengthened my ability to communicate technical concepts through visual storytelling.
