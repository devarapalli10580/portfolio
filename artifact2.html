Artifact 2: Evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning – Timeline Presentation

This artifact presents a comprehensive timeline capturing the historical progression and key milestones in AI and Machine Learning development.

Timeline Highlights

  • 1956: The Dartmouth Conference, birth of AI as a formal discipline.
  • 1980s: Rise of Expert Systems.
  • 1997: IBM’s Deep Blue defeats chess champion Garry Kasparov.
  • 2012: Breakthroughs in deep learning with AlexNet.
  • 2020s: Widespread adoption of AI in business, healthcare, and daily life.

Technologies Covered

  • Neural Networks
  • Deep Learning
