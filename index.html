Artifact 1: COVID-19 Vaccination Data Analysis

This artifact showcases my project analyzing COVID-19 vaccination data in India using RStudio. The project aimed to track vaccination progress, identify regional differences, and visualize trends effectively.

Objectives

  • Analyze vaccination coverage across Indian states and territories.
  • Create clear and impactful visualizations for stakeholders.
  • Deliver actionable insights to healthcare officials for targeted outreach.

Tools & Methodologies

  • R programming language (ggplot2, dplyr)
  • Data cleaning and preprocessing
  • Time-series visualization

Findings

The vaccination coverage varied significantly between urban and rural areas. Trends showed increasing uptake but highlighted regions requiring more focus.

Reflection

This artifact sharpened my skills in data analysis, visualization, and communicating technical findings to diverse audiences.

References

  • Government of India COVID-19 Open Data API
  • RStudio Documentation

Artifact 2: Evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Timeline

This interactive timeline highlights key milestones in AI and ML history from the 1950s to today. It uses color-coded eras and icons to differentiate waves of technological advancement and societal impact.

Key Milestones

  1. 1956: Dartmouth Conference — Birth of AI as a formal discipline
  2. 1960s: Early neural networks and perceptrons developed
  3. 1970s-80s: AI winters due to lack of progress and funding
  4. 1997: IBM Deep Blue beats chess champion Garry Kasparov
  5. 2012: Deep learning resurgence through ImageNet breakthroughs
  6. 2018: Introduction of Transformer architectures
  7. 2023: Generative AI models like GPT-4 revolutionize NLP

Tools Used

  • Canva and Google Slides for timeline design
  • Extensive research and critical analysis of AI history

Reflection

This artifact enhanced my strategic understanding of AI's evolution and its ethical and societal implications. It supports my technical learning by contextualizing current AI breakthroughs.
