Professional Portfolio Artifact 1

Introduction

Hello, my name is Naga Venkata Satyanarayana Devarapalli, and I am a graduate student pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence – Data Analytics at Indiana Wesleyan University. This artifact presents my work on an AI Lab project focused on developing a no-code chatbot assistant to empower users without programming skills.

This project involved ideation, design, and testing phases, emphasizing design thinking and prompt engineering. The goal was to simplify chatbot creation for non-technical users.

Artifact Description

Project: ChatBox Creator Bot on Mizou.com

This chatbot assistant guides users through creating custom chatbots using conversational steps, templates, tone selection, and visual flow design. It targets beginners and small business owners aiming to adopt AI chatbots quickly.

The platform removes technical barriers by enabling intuitive, no-code development.

Problem Solved

Many innovative individuals face hurdles building chatbots due to lack of coding skills. This AI assistant addresses that gap by offering visual instructions, examples, and walkthroughs to facilitate personalized chatbot creation.

Tools Used

  • Mizou.com (No-code chatbot platform)
  • React.js & Node.js (Frontend/backend)
  • Natural Language Processing APIs
  • AI prompt engineering techniques

Design Thinking Process

Empathize

Interviewed peers and business owners to identify chatbot creation challenges, focusing on interface complexity and jargon barriers.

Define

Formulated the challenge: How to enable non-coders to build chatbots easily?

Ideate

Brainstormed features like FAQ builders, tone selectors, flowchart previews, multilingual templates, and checklists.

Prototype

Built a working version with core features on Mizou with embedded documentation.

Test

Conducted peer tests and refined based on feedback, improving interaction flow and visual aids.

Reflection

This project taught me the value of user-centric design, interdisciplinary collaboration, and iterative improvement based on user feedback. Simplifying technical complexity into friendly AI experiences is key to adoption.

Feedback and Revisions

Feedback Area Initial Issue Action Taken Result
Language Complexity Users found the language too technical. Simplified to 8th-grade reading level, added analogies. User satisfaction increased significantly.
Visual Flow Confusing conversation flow. Added inline flowcharts and multimedia elements. Improved user engagement and understanding.
Onboarding New users felt lost initially. Implemented a welcome sequence with instructions. Higher completion rates and better feedback.

Professional Portfolio Artifact 4

Introduction

This artifact documents my experience with the Data Challenge Scenarios Coach, an AI chatbot designed to guide learners through realistic data challenges in machine learning. Through interactive dialogue, I explored issues such as data quality, bias, privacy, and security—developing problem-solving strategies relevant to the field.

Assignment Overview

The 4.3 assignment focused on understanding common data problems faced by data scientists and ML practitioners. The goal was to deepen knowledge of data processing, cleaning, sharing, privacy, and scalability challenges through active interaction with an AI coach.

Engaging with the chatbot allowed personalized, adaptive learning with real-time feedback and safe experimentation in a private environment.

Chatbot Interaction Experience

I accessed the Data Challenge Scenarios Coach via the provided SchoolAI link and engaged in a series of scenario-based dialogues. The AI posed challenging questions about data preparation, ethical use, and technical constraints, prompting me to reflect critically and apply ML concepts.

The chatbot’s instant feedback and clarifications supported progressive understanding, allowing me to explore difficult topics at my own pace and revisit concepts as needed.

Key Learnings

  • Data Processing & Cleaning: Identifying missing values, outliers, and normalization techniques.
  • Bias & Fairness: Recognizing sources of bias and mitigation strategies in datasets.
  • Privacy & Security: Understanding encryption, anonymization, and regulatory compliance (GDPR, FERPA).
  • Scalability Challenges: Managing large datasets and computational constraints.
  • Ethical AI Use: Balancing innovation with societal responsibility.

Challenges Addressed & Solutions

Handling Missing Data

Learned methods such as imputation, deletion, and model-based approaches for dealing with incomplete datasets.

Mitigating Dataset Bias

Explored sampling techniques, fairness-aware algorithms, and continuous monitoring to reduce bias impact.

Privacy-Preserving Data Sharing

Reviewed data anonymization, encryption, and policies to ensure compliance with data protection laws.

Ensuring Data Security

Studied best practices including access control, secure storage, and audit trails to protect sensitive information.

Reflection

This interactive experience enhanced my practical understanding of data challenges in ML workflows. It highlighted the importance of rigorous data management and ethical responsibility in AI projects.

Engaging with an AI coach strengthened my critical thinking and adaptive learning skills, preparing me for real-world AI deployments where data issues are inevitable.

Audience Value

  • Employers: Demonstrates my problem-solving abilities, data literacy, and ethical awareness crucial for AI roles.
  • Peers and Students: Provides insights into navigating practical data issues, supporting collaborative learning.
  • Instructors: Shows engagement with advanced learning tools and critical reflection on AI ethics and data challenges.
