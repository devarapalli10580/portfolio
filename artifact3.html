Artifact 3: SchoolAI-Assisted Learning Experience

This artifact explores the integration of AI-assisted learning tools within educational settings to improve student engagement, personalized learning, and outcomes.

Project Objectives

  • To implement AI tools that customize learning experiences based on individual student needs.
  • To measure the impact of AI interventions on student engagement and academic performance.
  • To identify challenges and opportunities in adopting AI in classroom environments.

Tools & Technologies

  • AI-based adaptive learning platforms (e.g., DreamBox, Smart Sparrow)
  • Data analytics for monitoring student progress
  • Natural Language Processing for automated feedback
  • Cloud-based learning management systems

Process & Implementation

  1. Initial needs assessment and stakeholder consultation.
  2. Selection of appropriate AI learning tools tailored for curriculum.
  3. Training educators on AI tool usage and data interpretation.
  4. Deployment in pilot classrooms with ongoing data collection.
  5. Analysis of outcomes and refinement of approach.

Outcomes & Reflections

The project demonstrated significant improvements in student engagement metrics and personalized learning efficacy. It also highlighted the importance of teacher involvement and ethical considerations in AI use.
