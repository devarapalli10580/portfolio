Artifact 3: SchoolAI-Assisted Learning Experience

Interactive Learning with AI-Powered Chatbot

Learning Experience Overview

This artifact documents my interactive learning experience with the SchoolAI chatbot, exploring machine learning training methods through inquiry-based dialogue. The session demonstrated critical thinking and the ability to extract knowledge through structured questioning.

Value Proposition: This artifact highlights my approach to AI-assisted learning, critical thinking, and deep understanding of ML concepts while using conversational AI as an educational tool.

  • Duration: 1 Week
  • Role: Researcher & Learner
  • Status: Completed

Key Learning Outcomes

  1. Supervised Learning: Understood pattern recognition from labeled datasets and applications like spam detection.
  2. Unsupervised Learning: Learned clustering methods such as k-means and their role in grouping data without labels.
  3. Reinforcement Learning: Explored trial-and-error learning with reward systems in robotics and gaming.
  4. Algorithm Importance: Recognized how algorithms act as recipes guiding model training.
  5. Data Quality: Emphasized the role of clean, labeled datasets for accurate AI performance.

Critical Thinking Demonstrated

  • Questioned AI reliability and risk in real-life applications.
  • Analyzed limitations of AI in adapting to changing patterns.
  • Compared human cognitive flexibility with AI performance.
  • Identified ethical concerns such as bias and fairness in AI models.

Challenges & Observations

Challenge: The AI skipped structured questions and demonstrated inconsistency.

Observation: Language confusion when switching between English and Hindi, revealing limitations in multilingual contexts.

Solution: Implemented follow-up prompts and critical questioning to ensure detailed responses.

Reflection

This exercise improved my understanding of ML concepts and highlighted the limitations of AI systems. It reinforced the importance of human oversight in AI-driven education and demonstrated how inquiry-based learning can be enhanced using AI tools.

Future Applications & Next Steps

  • Develop a News Authenticity Checker using supervised learning.
  • Experiment with deep learning and neural networks for advanced projects.
  • Focus on ethical AI design ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability.
