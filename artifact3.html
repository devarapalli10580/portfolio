My Learning Approach

Inquiry-Based Learning Strategy

I approached this AI-assisted learning session with a structured inquiry method, focusing on deep understanding rather than surface-level information gathering.

  • Progressive Questioning: Started with basic concepts and progressively asked for deeper explanations.
  • Real-world Applications: Consistently requested practical examples and use cases.
  • Critical Analysis: Questioned reliability, limitations, and potential failure scenarios.
  • Follow-up Questions: Used "explain in detail" and "give me examples" to deepen understanding.
  • Edge Case Exploration: Asked about negative scenarios and failure modes.

Critical Thinking Demonstrations

Key Critical Questions Asked:

Observation Description Impact
Question Skipping Behavior AI sometimes skipped questions without explanation. Caused confusion, showing incomplete interaction flow.
Language Confusion & Response Errors Occasional misunderstanding of phrasing and multi-language inputs. Led to inaccurate or irrelevant answers.
Rushing Behavior & Goal Orientation AI appeared focused on completing tasks quickly, sometimes at the cost of thoroughness. Reduced depth and quality of explanations.
Learning vs. Interface Confusion AI lacked ability to remember prior conversation context properly. Repeated similar answers, limiting adaptive learning.
